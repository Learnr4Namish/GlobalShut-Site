Features

GlobalShut has certain features that makes it unique. The features are as follows:-

Globally accessible

GlobalShut makes your computer accessible Globally by using LocalTunnel, that tunnels all the request to your computer

Free to use

GlobalShut is completely free of cost, that means you will be able to use GlobalShut in a hassle-free way

Consumes less electricity

Those who are bothered about electricity consumption, they can use GlobalShut in a hassle-free way because GlobalShut consumes very little or less energy.

Time of your choice

Scheduling a shutdown is as easy as you can think of! Shutdown time is your choice!

Highly-encrypted shutdown

Every shutdown request is highly encrypted. This means no third person has any access to your credentials other than the computer, GlobalShut's servers and you as a user.

Remote access from any device

Where there is browser and internet, there is GlobalShut client. Just use the link provided and control from any device. Moreover, you can get the files from your computer with ease using GlobalShut.
